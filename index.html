About Me

My name is Abhijeet Sainani. I was born on the 17th of November, 2002. I’ve spent most of my life living in the India in a city New Delhi. Some time ago I moved to Noida to study Animation at the Asian Acadamy of Film and Television. Being part of a team and working towards one big beautiful goal is the best thing about making the ideas. A group of people can accomplish so much more than just one single individual. At the start of the school year in 2021-2022 I started my training at the Asian Acadamy of Film and Television. I have been studying visual effects and Animation ever since. In my first year ( 2021/2022 ) I gained a lot of knowledge about the basics of animation. Included were lessons about photoshop, editing, sound design, graphics, web design, video editing, visual effects, 3D modeling and much more.

The software I like to work with:

Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere Pro, Affter Effects and 3DMax.
